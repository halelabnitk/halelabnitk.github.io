Hello There!

Healthcare Analytics & Language Engineering (HALE) Lab is a Research Lab situated in the Department of Information Technology, National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK), Surathkal.

We are a team of faculties, research scholars and students specializing in the research of Healthcare Analytics & Informatics and also Language and Linguistics using Natural Language Processing. Application of Artificial Intelligence for welfare of human beings for good healthcare delivery and services is our primary objective.

We hope that we can contribute towards a better healthcare for making people HALE and Hearty.
